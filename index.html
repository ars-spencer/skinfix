Your data

Everything you log — photos included — stays in this browser's local storage on this device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere, and publishing this app's code to GitHub does not publish your data. Back up regularly, since clearing browser data or switching devices/browsers will lose what isn't exported.

About this log

Built for tracking suspected papulopustular rosacea day to day — flare severity, possible triggers (heat, alcohol, spicy food, stress, sleep, dairy), and routine changes — so patterns are easier to see than they are to remember. Not a diagnostic tool, and not a substitute for an in-person dermatology visit.
